My Ball Pong Game

Ping pong and football combined? No way!

There is a football fun in each and everyone of us but it gets more fun if we combine it with a ping pong game. Follow the rules and don´t be afraid to play. Get ready!

The game

Your goal is to hit the ball everytime it´s near you and avoiding it to enter the goal and aiming towards the opponent goal. You will be playing against the console!

You can win but you can also lose

  • You win when you score 5
  • You loose when the opponent scores 5 or the clock timer reaches 0
